About Me

Hi, I'm Emmanuel.

I build web apps and websites that work well and feel good to use. Sometimes that's a simple landing page, other times it's a full-stack product with a lot going on under the hood.

I spend most of my time in React on the front end and Node.js on the back end, with a mix of databases depending on the project. What I care about most is shipping things that are reliable and easy to maintain, something that actually helps the people using it.

I've worked on projects that helped teams move faster and gave businesses real wins. I am happy working solo or with a team, as long as we are building something useful.

If you've got an idea or a project in motion, let's talk. Drop a note at ojighoroemmanuel@gmail.com or use the contact form below.
